# <ins>**New Main Weapons:**</ins>
## <ins>**MillE-Liter:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- A heavyweight Shooter
- Incredible range and damage
- Lackluster fire rate and ink coverage
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- MillE-Liter: Autobomb + Kraken Royale
- Axolotech MillE-Liter: Squid Beakon + Wave Breaker
- Unleaded MillE-Liter: Toxic Mist + Tenta Missiles
## <ins>**Samurai Roller:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Horizontal flick stats between Carbon and VRoller
- Vertical flick stats between Big Swig and VRoller
- Has a 'rev-up' time before actually rolling, but has the fastest roll of the class
- The design may be inspired by a [_certain_](https://splatoonwiki.org/wiki/Octo_Samurai) opponent from Octo Canyon
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Samurai Roller: Torpedo + Booyah Bomb
- Axolotech Samurai Roller: Splash Wall + Crab Tank
- Paper Samurai Roller: Toxic Mist + Triple Inkstrike
## <ins>**Scrap Dueller:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Has the shortest range in the class
- Intermediate charge time
- Longer than average charge hold
- Gets two 1-hit shots at full charge, but doesn't pierce
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Snipe Dueller 2S: Toxic Mist + Reefslider
- Snipe Dueller XVR: Suction Bomb + Ultra Stamp
- Scrappy Snipe Dueller: Torpedo + Triple Splashdown
## <ins>**Splitterator:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Borrows Machine's projectile
- Shots are smaller + weaker, but 2 are shot at the same time
- As the shots initially travel as 1, getting hit during this period totals more damage than Machine
- Shots travel without gravity
- At the end of their travel range, one shot will drop vertically down while the other will instead rise vertically up
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Splitterator: Burst Bomb + Killer Wail 5.1
- Axolotech Splitterator: Ink Mine + Big Bubbler
- Angular Splitterator: Autobomb + Ink Vac
## <ins>**Hyper Splatling:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Has range comparable to E-Liter
- Before reaching max charge has a somewhat slow fire rate and low damage
- At max charge, both fire rate and damage are increased
- Has unique Run Speed Up scaling, where 57ap will double the normal movement speed while firing and charging
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Hyper Splatling: Splash Wall + Super Chump
- Axolotech Hyper Splatling: Sprinkler + Big Bubbler
- Energised Hyper Splatling: Curling Bomb + Trizooka
## <ins>**Pulse Dualies:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Has low damage, range and accuracy, but high fire rate
- Temporarily gains perfect accuracy when jumping
- Rolls have a short delay, but then travel almost 2x Douser's distance
- Can shoot while rolling
- After rolling, fire rate slows down, but range, damage and accuracy increase greatly
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Pulse Dualies: Fizzy Bomb + Kraken Royale
- Axolotech Pulse Dualies: Burst Bomb + Big Bubbler
- Stereo Pulse Dualies: Angle Shooter + Ultra Stamp
## <ins>**Bubble Brella:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Slightly slower fire rate and lower damage than Undercover
- Can shoot with its shield open
- Movement slows down greatly when the shield is open
- Shield takes longer to open than Tent
- When open, the shield remains open for 4.5 seconds before breaking automatically
- When open , the shield fully surrounds the user and is invincible to most things
- Certain things can still destroy or bypass the shield
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Bubble Brella: Splat Bomb + Tenta Missiles
- Axolotech Bubble Brella: Curling Bomb + Killer Wail 5.1
- Big Bubble Brella: Fizzy Bomb + Zipcaster
## <ins>**Suds Blaster:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Very high fire rate but short range and low damage
- Does not have perfect accuracy when grounded
- Shots hang in the air when fired and explode about 1 second later
- Shot also explode on contact
- Shots normally have low damage, but can compound on one spot to deal increased damage in one blow
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Suds Blaster: Suction Bomb + Tenta Missiles
- Axolotech Suds Blaster: Splash Wall + Splattercolor Screen
- Squeaky Suds Blaster: Curling Bomb + Ink Vac
## <ins>**Abrushinator:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Very fast sliding speed
- Very thin ink trail
- Not slowed down by enemy ink while sliding
- Can 2 shot, but has less range than inkbrush and very quick damage falloff
- Becomes a 5 shot before inkbrush's falloff starts
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Abrushinator: Burst Bomb + Ink Vac
- Axolotech Abrushinator: Toxic Mist + Booyah Bomb
- Camo Abrushinator: Sprinkler + Splattercolor Screen
## <ins>**Snook Recurve:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- Has similar range, damage and charge time to Splat Charger
- Only has one arrow and one charge ring
- At max charge, the arrow can 1-hit an opponent
- If the arrow hits a surface, it will instantly explode with a radius slightly smaller than Explosher
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Snook Recurve: Splat Bomb + Ink Storm
- Axolotech Snook Recurve: Suction Bomb + Inkjet
- Golden Snook Recurve: Point Sensor + Zipcaster
## <ins>**8-Cyl:**</ins>
### <ins>**Stats:**</ins>
- A heavyweight Splatana
- The most range and highest damage of the class
- Slightly longer than average dash length
### <ins>**Kits:**</ins>
- Reflective 8-Cyl: Point Sensor + Super Chump
- Refractive 8-Cyl: Sprinkler + Trizooka
- Prismatic 8-Cyl: Angle Shooter+ Wave Breaker
